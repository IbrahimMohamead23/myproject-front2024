Expo City Eats - Delivering Delicacies in Expo City Dubai

Expo City Eats is an online food ordering platform that delivers food exclusively inside the gigantic and magnificent Expo City Dubai. It offers a wide variety of meal ordering options to its users with a hassle-free payment experience.

What is Expo City Eats?

Expo City Eats is a food ordering app developed specifically for the dwellers of Expo City Dubai. It provides users with the option to shuffle through various meals offered by the most popular restaurants in Dubai.

The online food delivery platform prioritizes convenience and offers users the ability to pay conveniently via Google Pay, Apple Pay, and Stripe.

How Does Expo City Eats Work?

The flow of the Expo City Eats app is similar to other popular online food ordering platforms. The user can choose to order food from a huge range of restaurants in Dubai. Each restaurant offers a slew of meals to choose from with some customization options.

Upon choosing a specific meal from a restaurant, the user pays for the same online using Apple Pay, Google Pay or Stripe. After successful payment, the user receives an order confirmation notification and the ability to track their order in real time.

The user is asked for feedback and to provide a rating for the order upon successful delivery. The user can leave reviews and ratings for the restaurant and the delivery partner.
